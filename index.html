About Us

A community built on faith, hope, and love

Our Story

Grace Community Church was founded in 1985 with a vision to create a welcoming space where people could encounter God's love and grow in their faith. What started as a small gathering of 20 families has grown into a vibrant community of over 500 members.

Today, we continue to be committed to worship, fellowship, discipleship, and service. We believe that the church is not just a building, but a family united in Christ's love.

Ministries

Find your place to connect, grow, and serve

Youth Ministry

Our youth ministry provides a safe and fun environment for students (grades 6-12) to grow in their faith, build friendships, and discover their God-given purpose. We meet every Friday at 7:00 PM for games, worship, and Bible study.

Learn More

Kids Ministry

We believe that children matter to God and to us! Our Kids Ministry offers age-appropriate programs for infants through 5th grade during Sunday services. Through engaging activities, worship, and Bible stories, children learn about God's love in a caring environment.

Learn More

Small Groups

Small groups are the heartbeat of our church community. These intimate gatherings of 8-12 people meet weekly in homes throughout the area for Bible study, prayer, and authentic fellowship. Join a group to experience deeper connections and spiritual growth.

Learn More

Missions

We're passionate about sharing God's love both locally and globally. Our missions ministry supports missionaries around the world and organizes local outreach projects. From serving at homeless shelters to international mission trips, there are many ways to get involved.
